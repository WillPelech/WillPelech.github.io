Learning Transformers From Scratch

2026 · in progress
This post is a work in progress — I'll fill it in as I go.

I've been working through transformer architecture from the ground up — not just using HuggingFace and calling .generate(), but actually understanding what's happening inside. These are my notes.

Why bother?

After my internship at Datadog working on AI agent infrastructure, I realized I could build reliable systems around LLMs without fully understanding the models themselves. That bothered me. I want to know what attention is actually computing, why positional encodings work, and what the residual stream is doing. The goal isn't to train frontier models — it's to have a real mental model of the thing I'm building on top of.

Starting point
